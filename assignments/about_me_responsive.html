My Experience

I completed my first degree in Teaching English as a Second Language in 2019. With a minor in Japanese and a strong understanding of linguistics, my goal was to become an English instructor in Japan, but the COVID-19 pandemic put a hold on that plan. Spending more time at home teaching remotely (and throwing in the occasional gaming session), I started thinking about how the laptop in front of me worked behind the scenes.

My interest in linguistics led me to free tutorials and classes teaching the basics of programming, and I soon found myself returning to Kent State University to get a bachelor's degree in computer science. I learned useful skills needed in a wide variety of computer science fields, my main focus being video game development. With experience in C++, C#, and game engines like Unity and Unreal, I hope to build a long career giving back to the gaming community.
